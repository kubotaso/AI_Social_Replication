## Table 3 (target descriptive frequency table)
**Title:** *Table 3. Frequency Distributions for Attitude toward 18 Music Genres: General Social Survey, 1993*

### Column categories (response options)
For **each music genre**, the table reports frequencies for these **attitude response categories**:

1. **(1) Like very much**
2. **(2) Like it**
3. **(3) Mixed feelings**
4. **(4) Dislike it**
5. **(5) Dislike very much**
6. **(M) Don’t know much about it**
7. **(M) No answer**
8. **Mean** (mean of the 1–5 attitude scale; “Don’t know”/“No answer” are marked as missing)

### Row categories (items)
Rows are the **attitude categories** listed above; the **items being summarized** are the 18 **music genres** (each genre is a separate column in the table). The genres are:

1. Latin/Salsa  
2. Jazz  
3. Blues/R&B  
4. Show Tunes  
5. Oldies  
6. Classical/Chamber  
7. Reggae  
8. Swing/Big Band  
9. New Age/Space  
10. Opera  
11. Bluegrass  
12. Folk  
13. Pop/Easy Listening  
14. Contemporary Rock  
15. Rap  
16. Heavy Metal  
17. Country/Western  
18. Gospel  

### Summary statistics shown
- **Frequencies (counts)** for each response option (categories 1–5 plus the two missing categories).
- **Mean** attitude score (on the 1–5 scale) for each genre.  
- **No percentages** are displayed in Table 3.
