Figure 2: Party Identification Among Presidential Voters and Nonvoters
Title: "Proportions of (Strong or Weak) Identifiers in National Election Study Sample"
One panel with two lines:
- Voters (filled circles, solid line): proportion of (Strong+Weak) identifiers among presidential voters
- Nonvoters (open circles, dashed line): proportion of (Strong+Weak) identifiers among presidential nonvoters
X-axis: 1956 to 1996 (presidential election years only)
Y-axis: 0.0 to 1.0
Only presidential election years: 1952, 1956, 1960, 1964, 1968, 1972, 1976, 1980, 1984, 1988, 1992, 1996
Data: For each presidential year, among those with valid VCF0301:
- Voters: VCF0706 in {1,2,3,4} or VCF0702=2 (voted). Compute proportion with VCF0301 in {1,2,6,7}
- Nonvoters: VCF0706=7 or VCF0702=1 (did not vote). Compute proportion with VCF0301 in {1,2,6,7}
