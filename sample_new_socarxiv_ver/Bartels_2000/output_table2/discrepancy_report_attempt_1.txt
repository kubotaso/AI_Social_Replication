ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS:
The coefficient discrepancies in 1960 and 1974 are likely due to data vintage differences.
The ANES CDF has been revised multiple times between 1995 and 2019. Some respondents may
have been recoded, added, or removed. These differences are inherent to the data version
and cannot be fixed by changing the analysis code.

The log-likelihood differences are directly caused by N differences - more/fewer observations
change the total log-likelihood even if the model is specified identically.
