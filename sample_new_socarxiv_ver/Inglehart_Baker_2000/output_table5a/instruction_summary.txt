Variable Mapping for Table 5a

DV: Traditional/Secular-Rational factor score from Table 1 (nation-level).

IVs:
1. Real GDP per capita, 1980 (in $1,000s U.S.): World Bank NY.GDP.PCAP.PP.KD, year ~1980, divide by 1000.
2. % employed in industrial sector, 1980: World Bank SL.IND.EMPL.ZS, year ~1980.
3. % enrolled in education: World Bank enrollment rates (SE.PRM.ENRR + SE.SEC.ENRR + SE.TER.ENRR), year ~1980. May be combined or use total.
4. % employed in service sector, 1980: World Bank SL.SRV.EMPL.ZS, year ~1980.
5. Ex-Communist dummy (=1): See Table 4 zone assignments.
6. Historically Catholic dummy (=1): France, Belgium, Italy, Spain, Portugal, Austria, Ireland, Poland, Croatia, Slovenia, Hungary, Czech Republic, Slovakia, Latin American countries, Philippines.
7. Historically Confucian dummy (=1): Japan, South Korea, Taiwan, China.

MODEL SPECIFICATIONS:
- Model 1: GDP + Industrial
- Model 2: GDP + Industrial + Education enrollment + Service sector + Education (N=46 due to missing education data)
- Model 3: GDP + Industrial + Ex-Communist
- Model 4: GDP + Industrial + Catholic
- Model 5: GDP + Industrial + Confucian
- Model 6: GDP + Industrial + Ex-Communist + Catholic + Confucian

PROCEDURE:
1. Compute factor scores, merge with World Bank data.
2. Run OLS for each model specification.
3. Report unstandardized coefficients, standard errors, Adjusted R².
4. Use .venv/bin/python with statsmodels OLS.

NOTE: These are UNSTANDARDIZED coefficients (not standardized like Table 4).
