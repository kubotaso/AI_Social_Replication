Data Requirements for Inglehart & Baker (2000) Replication
==========================================================

1. PRIMARY DATASET: World Values Survey (WVS) Longitudinal Data
   - Source: World Values Survey Association (www.worldvaluessurvey.org)
   - Waves needed: Wave 1 (1981-1982), Wave 2 (1990-1991), Wave 3 (1995-1998)
   - Also includes European Values Survey (EVS) 1990 data for 15 European societies
   - 65 societies total
   - Individual-level microdata needed (not aggregated)
   - Total N approximately 165,594 (nation-level analysis uses N=65)

   Required WVS Variables:
   ----------------------
   Traditional vs. Secular-Rational Dimension (5 items):
   - A006: Importance of God in respondent's life (10-point scale)
   - A042: Child qualities: obedience vs independence/determination
   - F120: Justifiability of abortion (1-10 scale)
   - G006: National pride
   - E018: Respect for authority

   Survival vs. Self-Expression Dimension (5 items):
   - Y002: Materialist/Postmaterialist values (4-item index)
   - A008: Feeling of happiness
   - E025/E026: Petition signing / Would not sign petition
   - F118: Justifiability of homosexuality (1-10 scale)
   - A165: Interpersonal trust (most people can be trusted vs. can't be too careful)

   Additional variables for Tables 2 & 3 (correlations):
   - A001: Importance of family
   - A002: Importance of friends
   - A003: Importance of leisure
   - A004: Importance of religion
   - A005: Importance of work
   - A029-A042: Child qualities (various)
   - A165: Interpersonal trust
   - C001-C002: Job motivation items
   - D054: Woman earning more than husband
   - D057-D059: Gender role items
   - E012-E015: Political action items
   - E023: Interest in politics
   - E035-E042: Confidence in institutions
   - E114-E117: Democracy/government items
   - F028: Believe in heaven
   - F051: Believe in hell
   - F034: Religious person
   - F063: Church attendance
   - F050: Comfort from religion
   - F114-F121: Justifiability items (various)
   - G007: Protectionism
   - S002: Survey wave identifier
   - S003: Country code
   - S020: Year of survey
   - X001: Sex
   - X002: Age / year of birth
   - X003: Age
   - X007: Marital status
   - X025: Education level

   For Table 4 (cultural zone regressions):
   - Country-level factor scores on both dimensions
   - Cultural zone classifications (9 zones based on Huntington)

   For Tables 5a & 5b (modernization regressions):
   - Country-level factor scores
   - External economic/structural data (see below)

   For Table 6 (church attendance):
   - F063: Church attendance frequency, by country and wave

   For Table 7 (importance of God):
   - A006: Importance of God (% rating "10"), by country and wave

   For Table 8 (meaning of life):
   - F063 or similar: "Often think about meaning and purpose of life", by country and wave

   For Figures 7 & 8 (birth cohort analysis):
   - X003: Age or X002: Year of birth
   - Factor scores computed at individual level
   - Society type classification (4 groups)

2. SUPPLEMENTARY DATA: Economic and Structural Indicators
   - Source: Penn World Tables / World Bank
   - Real GDP per capita, 1980 (in $1,000s U.S., PPP estimates)
   - GNP per capita, 1995 (World Bank PPP, U.S. dollars) - for Figure 2 & 4
   - Percentage employed in industrial sector, 1980
   - Percentage employed in service sector, 1980
   - Percentage enrolled in primary, secondary, and tertiary education
   - Life expectancy at birth, 1995 (for cohort analysis)

   These will be obtained via World Bank API (wbgapi Python package).
