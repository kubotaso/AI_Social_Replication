Figure 6: Change Over Time in Location on Two Dimensions of Cross Cultural Variation for 38 Societies

Type: Scatter plot with ARROWS showing change from earlier to later survey.
X-axis: Survival/Self-Expression Dimension (-2.0 to 2.0)
Y-axis: Traditional/Secular-Rational Dimension (-2.2 to 1.8)

Each society has an arrow from its initial survey position to its latest survey position.
Open circles = Initial survey, Filled circles = Last survey.

38 countries with time-series data (at least 2 time points in WVS waves 1-3).

Key patterns visible:
- Most advanced industrial societies moved upper-right (more secular-rational, more self-expression)
- Ex-Communist societies split: some moved upper-right, others moved lower-left
- Russia, Belarus moved left (less self-expression) after Communist collapse

Countries shown include labels like:
"East Germany 97", "Japan 95", "West Germany 81", "Sweden 96", "Norway 81", etc.
Labels include the survey year.

Note: The 38 countries included are those for which time-series data are available from the earliest to the latest time points in the World Values Surveys.
