Discrepancy Report - Attempts 11-12
Attempt 11 (4-item linear conversion + all): Score 75
Attempt 12 (3-item + all): Score 76 (ties with attempt 10)

Key findings from systematic testing:
1. All "all observations" variants tie at 76 regardless of autonomy construction
2. The score is constrained by:
   - Autonomy loading always ~0.70-0.75 at nation (paper: 0.89) -> 4 pts lost
   - Ordering always fails -> 10 pts lost
   - Variance always off by ~5% (nation) -> 2-4 pts lost
3. Maximum achievable score with current data: ~86 (if all PARTIALs become MATCHes)
4. But practical ceiling is ~78-80 due to interdependence of loadings

Country exclusion analysis showed:
- Excluding AZE, SRB, or AUS gives score 80 (gains ordering pts)
- But these countries were in the original study, so exclusion is not justified

The score plateau at 76 appears to be a data limitation.
The WVS v5 harmonized dataset differs from the original 1990s data
that Inglehart & Baker used.
