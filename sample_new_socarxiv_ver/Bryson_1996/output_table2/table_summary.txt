Table 2: Standardized OLS Coefficients from the Regression of the Number of Music Genres Disliked on Racism and Demographic Variables
General Social Survey, 1993

Structure: 2 OLS regression models, all with STANDARDIZED coefficients

Model 1: Dislike of Rap, Reggae, Blues/R&B, Jazz, Gospel, and Latin Music
  (6 genres disproportionately liked by Hispanic or Black respondents)

Model 2: Dislike of the 12 Remaining Genres

Notes:
- All coefficients are STANDARDIZED (beta weights)
- Model 1 DV: count of "dislike" + "dislike very much" for rap, reggae, blues, jazz, gospel, latin (range 0-6)
- Model 2 DV: count of "dislike" + "dislike very much" for the remaining 12 genres (range 0-12)
  The 12 remaining: musicals, oldies, classicl, bigband, newage, opera, blugrass, folk, moodeasy, conrock, hvymetal, country
- The 6 "minority" genres are those disproportionately liked by Black or Hispanic respondents
- The constant shown is UNSTANDARDIZED
