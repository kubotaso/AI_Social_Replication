Data Requirements for Bryson (1996) Replication
=============================================

Dataset: General Social Survey (GSS), 1993
Source: NORC at the University of Chicago
N: ~1,606 respondents (full sample); varies by model due to missing data

Required Variables:
------------------

DEPENDENT VARIABLES:
- Musical exclusiveness: Count of "dislike" and "dislike very much" responses across 18 music genres
  (Likert scale: 1=Like very much, 2=Like it, 3=Mixed feelings, 4=Dislike it, 5=Dislike very much, M=Don't know, M=No answer)
- Sub-counts: Dislike of 6 minority-associated genres (rap, reggae, blues/R&B, jazz, gospel, Latin/salsa)
- Sub-counts: Dislike of 12 remaining genres

18 MUSIC GENRE VARIABLES (each on 1-5 Likert scale):
1. Latin/Salsa
2. Jazz
3. Blues/R&B
4. Show Tunes
5. Oldies
6. Classical/Chamber
7. Reggae
8. Swing/Big Band
9. New Age/Space
10. Opera
11. Bluegrass
12. Folk
13. Easy Listening
14. Pop/Contemporary Rock
15. Rap
16. Heavy Metal
17. Country/Western
18. Gospel

INDEPENDENT VARIABLES:
- Education (years of schooling)
- Household income (per capita)
- Occupational prestige
- Female (gender dummy)
- Age
- Black (race dummy)
- Hispanic (ethnicity dummy)
- Other race
- Conservative Protestant
- No religion
- Southern (region)
- Political intolerance (count of intolerant responses to 15 civil liberties questions)
- Racism scale (composite of racial attitude questions, range 0-5)

RACISM SCALE ITEMS (dichotomous):
1. "Would you object to sending your children to a school where more than half the children are Black?"
2. "Do you favor or oppose the busing of Black and White school children?"
3. "On average Blacks have worse jobs, income, and housing than White people. Do you think these differences are mainly due to discrimination?"
4. "...because most Blacks don't have the chance for education that it takes to rise out of poverty?"
5. "...because most Blacks just don't have the motivation or will power to pull themselves up out of poverty?"

POLITICAL INTOLERANCE ITEMS:
- 15 dichotomous questions about willingness to allow 5 groups (anti-religious, racist, communist, militarist, homosexual) to:
  (1) make a speech in your community
  (2) teach in a college or university
  (3) have a book in your public library
- Scale range: 0-15

Data Acquisition Method:
- R package: gssr (from kjhealy r-universe)
- Will extract 1993 GSS data and save as CSV
