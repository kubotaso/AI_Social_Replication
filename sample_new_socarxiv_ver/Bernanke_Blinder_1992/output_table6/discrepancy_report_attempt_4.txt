FUNDAMENTAL DIAGNOSIS:
The core problem is WEAK INSTRUMENTS due to data vintage differences.
With current (revised) data:
- Contemporaneous correlations between macro and NBR innovations are very small
- First-stage F-stats are 0.6-4.2, all below the weak instrument threshold of ~10
- This makes 2SLS estimates highly unstable and biased

The paper's original DRI data likely had:
- Different revision vintages for industrial production, employment, reserves
- Stronger contemporaneous correlation structure
- Potentially different seasonal adjustment methods

OBSERVATION: The paper's qualitative finding IS reproduced: all slopes
are negative and statistically insignificant. The quantitative magnitudes
differ due to data vintage, but the economic conclusion is the same.
