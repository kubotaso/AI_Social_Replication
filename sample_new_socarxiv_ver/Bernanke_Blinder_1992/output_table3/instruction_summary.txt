Variable mapping:
Forecasted variables (same as Tables 1-2):
- Industrial production → log_industrial_production
- Capacity utilization → log_capacity_utilization
- Employment → log_employment
- Unemployment rate → unemp_male_2554
- Housing starts → log_housing_starts
- Personal income → log_personal_income_real
- Retail sales → log_retail_sales_real
- Consumption → log_consumption_real
- Durable-goods orders → log_durable_goods_real (MISSING)

RHS variables:
- CPI → log_cpi
- M1 → log_m1
- M2 → log_m2
- CPBILL → cpbill_long (= cpaper_6m_long - tbill_6m, from bb1992_data.csv)
- TERM → term (= treasury_10y - treasury_1y)
- FUNDS → funds_rate

Model specification:
- OLS regression
- Constant + 6 lags of dep var + 6 lags CPI + 6 lags M1 + 6 lags M2 + 6 lags CPBILL + 6 lags TERM + 6 lags FUNDS
- Total regressors: 1 + 6*7 = 43
- F-test p-values for each column variable

Sample: 1961-07-01 to 1989-12-01

Dataset: bb1992_data.csv
