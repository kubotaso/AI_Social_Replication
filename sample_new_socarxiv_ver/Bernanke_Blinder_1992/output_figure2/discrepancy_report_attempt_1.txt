=== DIAGNOSIS ===
The large discrepancy in the inflation response is likely due to:
1. Data vintage effects: CPI data has been significantly revised since 1992.

2. Possible different CPI series: The paper may have used a different CPI
   measure (e.g., CPI-U vs CPI-W, or CPI less food and energy).
