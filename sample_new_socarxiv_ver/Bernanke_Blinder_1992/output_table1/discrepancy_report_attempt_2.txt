DIAGNOSIS:
The M2 issue is clearly a data vintage problem. The FRED M2 series has been revised
substantially since 1992 (money market fund reclassification, savings deposit
reclassification in 2020, etc.). The revised M2 data has different dynamics than the
original DRI data used by Bernanke and Blinder.

The BOND discrepancies for Employment (Panel A) and Retail sales (Panel B) suggest
possible issues with the 10-year Treasury data as well.
