2. STANDARD ERRORS: The paper uses Murphy-Topel (1985) correction for two-step SE.
   Our naive OLS SEs are ~0.0006 while the paper reports ~0.03. The correction
   inflates SEs by ~50x to account for first-step estimation error.

   The Murphy-Topel correction: V_2_corrected = V_2 + V_2*(C - R)*V_1*(C - R)'*V_2
   where V_1 is step 1 variance, V_2 is step 2 variance,
   C = gradient of step 2 moment condition w.r.t. step 1 parameters,
   R = cross-step information.
