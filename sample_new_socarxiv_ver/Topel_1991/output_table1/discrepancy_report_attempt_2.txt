DIAGNOSIS:
The log wage change means are systematically MORE NEGATIVE than the paper.
This is consistent across all tenure bins and the total.
The discrepancy increases with tenure (0.020 for 6-10 to 0.074 for 21+).
Workers with more tenure tend to have been displaced earlier, requiring more deflation.
This suggests the deflator values we use create a larger inflation adjustment
than the vintage values used by Topel in the late 1980s.
