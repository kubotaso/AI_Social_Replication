ROOT CAUSE ANALYSIS:
The fundamental issues are:
A) R-squared gap (0.49 vs 0.42): Topel may use a different wage deflation,
   or the data sample differs (1968-83 vs our 1971-83). GNP deflation gives
   R2=0.28, CPS gives 0.49. Neither matches 0.42.
B) Interaction terms: The ct*tenure interaction has extreme multicollinearity
   with tenure^2 (corr=0.958). The coefficient sign and magnitude are very
   sensitive to the exact specification and sample.
C) Experience^2 mismatch: Our experience measure (age - education - 6) may
   differ from Topel's. He may use direct labor market entry age or pre-sample
   experience from PSID employment history.
