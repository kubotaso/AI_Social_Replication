=== KEY DISCOVERY ===
The dataset contains a tenure_mos column with PSID-reported tenure in months,
available for years 1976-1977 and 1980-1983. This provides pre-panel tenure
information (e.g., a person with tenure_topel=4 might have tenure_mos=120,
meaning 10 years total tenure). The max completed tenure using this data is
38 years (vs 13 for panel-only ct_obs).

However, naive use of this data scored 84 due to:
1. tenure_mos only available for 6 of 13 years (1976-77, 1980-83)
2. Values of 0 are ambiguous (missing vs < 1 year)
3. Mixing tenure_mos-based and panel-based tenure creates inconsistencies
4. The censor interaction becomes highly significant (*** vs target ns)

A proper implementation would require:
- Careful cross-referencing of tenure_mos across multiple years per job
- Proper handling of 0 and 999 (top-coded) values
- Consistent tenure construction for all person-year observations
- Understanding PSID questionnaire changes across waves

This is beyond the scope of the current iteration but confirms the
hypothesis that the paper used pre-panel tenure data.
